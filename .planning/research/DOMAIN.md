## Findings

- The strongest portfolio story is sales operations acceleration: turn messy inbound inquiries into structured, follow-up-ready opportunities.
- The existing codebase already contains the domain depth needed for a credible demo: ingestion, statuses, follow-ups, custom fields, calendar, reports, forecasts, and assistant logs.
- The main risks are environment setup, database dependency, unsafe test names, and drifting into real integrations before publication.
- Controlled assistant actions fit the human-in-the-loop positioning better than autonomous mutation.

## Planning Implications

- Phase 1 should prove the core lead workflow with safe demo data.
- Phase 2 should verify supporting views and assistant safety.
- Phase 3 should focus on documentation and deployment readiness.
