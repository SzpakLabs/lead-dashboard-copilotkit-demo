# Plan 02: Harden Assistant Reports And Forecast Safety

<objective>
Verify and fix assistant availability, typed tool contracts, mutation confirmation, report rendering, and forecast assumption labeling without replacing CopilotKit or adding a new assistant framework.
</objective>

<must_haves>
<truths>

- D-14: CopilotKit remains the assistant framework.
- D-15: mutation tools preview first, apply only after confirmation, and log assistant actions.
- D-16: missing credentials produce a clean disabled state.
- D-11 and D-12: forecast values and assumptions are explicit and deterministic.
- D-18: availability answers use dashboard-only limitation framing.
</truths>
</must_haves>

<threat_model>

- T-02-04: Missing assistant credentials could crash the app or expose confusing assistant chrome. Mitigation: preserve readiness gating in `src/lib/assistant/config.ts` and page-level optional rendering.
- T-02-05: Assistant could mutate lead data without confirmation. Mitigation: preserve preview/apply schemas, `confirm_assistant_mutation`, and `assistantActionLogs`.
- T-02-06: Reports or forecasts could overstate uncertain revenue. Mitigation: keep confirmed, weighted, optimistic, assumptions, and missing-data notes separate.
</threat_model>
